What is Couple Bedtime Stories?

Is a project to bring more intimacy to our long distance relationship. Being far apart, and at times go half the day without hearing your voice, can bring strain onto the relationship. The aim is to have those late-night connections remain, no matter the distance, through storytelling.

Falling asleep on the phone to your voice, is the best feeling to wake up to in the morning. With Audio bedtime stories, I've read them in advance, and if we find ourselves late at work, being caught up with friends, or just too busy, intimacy can remain.
